About Us

TransLogistics is a sole proprietorship, full-service 3rd party logistics provider owned by Gregory Frazier, a veteran with over 20 years of specialized experience in global transportation and logistics solutions. We offer a full range of services from single (LTL), truckload and intermodal moves to multiple shipments from and to multiple locations. We specialize in helping you to cover difficult lanes and controlling your freight costs.


Mission Statement

Our mission is to build meaningful partnerships with our customers to provide professional, quality transportation and logistics services.



Vision Statement

Our Vision is to become a leading, recognized, veteran-owned Transportation/Logistics Supplier brand in the freight brokerage industry that exists to serve as a valuable liaison between suppliers, customers, and owners-operators on a global scale within a quality, service-based firm that exceeds the expectations or our customers.

About the CEO & Founder

Gregory Frazier (Greg), MBA, LSS, DSL

Gregory Frazier is the CEO of TransLogistics, LLC and a Military Veteran with 20+ years of experience in global Transportation & Logistics Management. As CEO of TransLogistics, he is responsible for operations strategies of the company. Greg also develops cost-effective solutions for efficient/effective transportation and logistics services. He has a Master’s in Business Administration/Project Management, Lean Six Sigma and Fleet Management certification, and knowledge of related automated systems.
