Products & Services

Freight Management Movement (Local & OTR)

We provides professional Freight Management Movement for local and over the road freight movement. We can offer our customers the appropriate equipment, planning, organization, documents and custom clearance management to meet shipping requirements. We will source through our carrier base to find the most competitive pricing and transit time to make sure your shipment arrives at its destination in mint condition in perfect conditions and on time to its final destination.



Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Providing shipping service to accommodate relatively small loads or quantities of freight.


Truckload (TL)

Providing shipping mode by carriers for a dedicated shipment at the most competitive cost.

Dry Van

Coordinates the most common and versatile type of freight with the most reliable dry van owner operators across United States. Connects reliable carriers to support our customers dry van freight safety and efficiently.

Temperature Controlled

Arranges temperature controlled transport for freight that are sensitive to climate conditions and shipped at specifications with prompt, on-time delivery. Connect freight with the top, most reliable carriers at the most competitive rates and reliability to meet our customers reefer requirements.

Flatbed

Connects the proper flatbed equipment to accommodate the load for shipping/receiving points that may not have an available dock at their disposal and may not be easily transported in a dry van. Understanding that flatbed facilitation is a versatile option for many shippers, we will be happy to accommodate any needs you may have.

Specialized Freight

Provides mode of carrier for freight large, weight, dimensions, or demand special handling services during transit safely and efficiently. Translogostics will identify the most qualified carrier for your shipment, verifying accredited on-time record on export, import, and rail loads in order to ensure a safe transition outbound and/or safe delivery inbound.
